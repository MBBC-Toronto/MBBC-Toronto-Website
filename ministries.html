Meet Our Original Composers

× See Original Compositions

(From left to right) Sis. Glore Fe Abotanatin, Sis. Venjiemeh Paine,


The rich tapestry of Christian worship is woven together with hymns and sacred music, which have played an integral role in the life of the Church for centuries. The music of the Church serves as a testament to the enduring power of faith and creativity. As we sing these Godly music, we pay tribute to the original composers who have enriched our spiritual lives and remind us that their timeless melodies remain an integral part of our worship heritage. Their compositions continue to resonate with the faithful, offering solace, inspiration, and a profound connection to the divine.
