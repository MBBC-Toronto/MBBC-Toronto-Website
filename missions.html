Acts 1:8 KJV

But ye shall receive power, after that the Holy Ghost is come upon you: and ye shall be witnesses unto me both in Jerusalem, and in all Judaea, and in Samaria,

and unto the uttermost part of the earth.

The Bible underscores the importance of missions as a central aspect of the Christian faith. It calls upon believers to go forth and share the Gospel, make disciples, serve others in love, and be willing vessels for God's mission in the world. These teachings emphasize that missions are not only a command but also a privilege and a profound expression of God's love for all people.
